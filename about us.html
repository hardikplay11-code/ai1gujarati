Who Are We?

Ai1 Gujarati is a homegrown snack brand inspired by the rich culture, flavours, and traditions of Gujarat. We believe that every bite should bring warmth, nostalgia, and pure satisfaction. Our snacks are handmade in small batches to ensure freshness and authenticity.

What started as a small idea in the kitchen soon became a brand trusted by families across India. With love for homemade snacks and passion for quality, we turned traditional recipes into professionally crafted products while keeping the original taste alive.
